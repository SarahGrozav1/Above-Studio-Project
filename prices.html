Our team can offer you the following services

Hire Us

Online

(80 - 100 EUR/hour)

The plan of the space that you are going to arrange is sent by e-mail together with some photos that show its current state. You will receive from us a questionnaire-type form to fill in, to help us understand what your needs and wishes are related to the project. If there are aspects you want to mention and they are not in the questionnaire, you can write them separately from it in the same e-mail. We set the date and time of the consultation together and meet on zoom. After the consultation, you will receive an e-mail with the summary of the discussion and some sketched essential ideas, a list with suggestions of suppliers and some reference pictures for obtaining visual information; The payment for this service is paid before the start of the consultation.

In special stores

(130 - 200 EUR/hour)

In Dubai. Additional travel costs are charged for locations outside the city. Travel to specialty stores will be provided by the customer. Together, we arrange a visit to the location you arrange, where we discuss each area in detail. Based on the verbal discussions, we establish a list of the necessary products and the specialty stores where we will go. We choose all the objects, take photos, and you will receive the list of what we chose together by e-mail together with the summary of the discussion, so you will be able to purchase easily afterwards all the products. Payment for this service is paid on the spot.

Consulting during
the development process

(80 - 130 EUR/hour)

Do you have a space that is already furnished but you feel that you want to make small adjustments or additions and you need help? Together we set up an online or on-site consultation session where, based on your needs and wishes and on the basis of the allocated budget, we establish verbal solutions and ideas, a list of suppliers and select some reference pictures to obtain visual information.
