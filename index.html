About Us

Out of the passion and desire to transform the spaces
we live in or frequent into places with a welcoming and
comfortable environment that fulfills the purpose after
the transformation, we decided to found Above Studio, an
interior design studio, in 2017."
Designing a space does not only require a visual approach,
but it is about the process of thinking itself, about skills,
aspects that helped us define as our professional goal the improvement
of the quality and reality of our clients' lives through the projects
we work on.
